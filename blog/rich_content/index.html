Gunfu Heroes

A 2-4 local multiplayer party game

Background

In the year 21xx, the show GunFu Heroes’ ratings have declined. In a desperate ploy to revitalize the show’s former glory, the show’s executives have started “recruiting” fighters from across both time and space. Prove your worth in the arena with an array of weapons and feed the corporate machine with the spoils of war.

So what is it?

GunFu Heroes is a quick-paced, local multiplayer party game where your goal is to be the last one standing. A variety of weapons introduce different styles of play while various fighters and maps keep the game feeling fresh. Whether you want casual fun or competitive showdowns, GunFu Heroes offers gripping 2-4 player combat.

Features:

  • 2-4 Player Couch Combat!
  • Full Keyboard and Controller Support
  • At Least One Fruit
  • Play With a Teammate or Fight Alone
  • Easy to Learn, Difficult to Master…
